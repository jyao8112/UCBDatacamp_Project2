Find restaurants!

Welcome to our SF restaurant finder app! We use a Yelp dataset with over 1000 local San Francisco restaurants. Our landing page features a layered interactive map which shows markers for restaurants based on rating, popularity, price, and favorite restaurants selected by the users. Hover over our plotly charts to see a visual distribution of different restaurant categories and price range. Our restaurant finder page allows you to select a category of restaurants, which then generates an interactive list of all restaurants in that category as well as a map of the restaurant location and the ten closest parking meters and their real time availability status.
